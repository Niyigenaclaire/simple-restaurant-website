Kimironko Market

Kimironko Market is the biggest market in Kigali. You can buy many things here.

What You Can Buy

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Clothes and shoes
  • Traditional fabrics
  • Handmade crafts
  • Food and spices

Best Days to Visit

  1. Saturday - Most vendors are there
  2. Wednesday - Fresh produce day
  3. Friday - Good for shopping
