Kimironko Market

Kigali's Vibrant Shopping Experience

Kimironko Market is the largest and most vibrant market in Kigali. From fresh produce to traditional crafts, you'll find everything you need in this bustling marketplace.

What You Can Buy

Fresh Produce

Fruits, vegetables, and herbs

Clothes & Shoes

Fashion for all ages

Traditional Fabrics

Beautiful African prints

Handmade Crafts

Unique souvenirs

Food & Spices

Local ingredients

Home Goods

Kitchenware and decor

Best Days to Visit

1

Saturday

Most vendors are present - busiest day with the best selection

2

Wednesday

Fresh produce day - best time for fruits and vegetables

3

Friday

Great for shopping - good variety and less crowded

Market Sections
