Kigali Genocide Memorial

A Place of Remembrance and Learning

The Kigali Genocide Memorial is a place to remember and learn about Rwanda's history. It honors the memory of those who lost their lives and educates visitors about peace and reconciliation.

What You Can See

  • Museum with photos and stories
  • Beautiful memorial gardens
  • Memorial walls with names
  • Education center and library
  • Children's memorial
